Description

Automated epilepsy diagnosis based on artificial intelligence (AI) aims to relieve medical professionals' manual identification, prediction, and treatment of seizure and epilepsy-specific EEG signals. The importance of 2D EEG frames extracted through various processing pipelines over 1D signal analysis using various CNN architectures like AlexNet and LeNet has been underlined in existing research in this field. This rhythmicity spectrogram picture dataset was created using the CHB-MIT EEG scalp database. Seizure and non-seizure EEG images have been added to the dataset's train, test, and validate files in png format. For the detection of seizure and non-seizure EEG pictures, it can be included into machine learning and deep learning pipelines.


Dataset of rhythmicity spectrogram based images of seizure and non-seizure EEG signals
